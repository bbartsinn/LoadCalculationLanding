Common questions

Calgary and Edmonton electrical load calculation FAQ

Is this only for secondary suites?

No. It is also useful for laneway suites, EV chargers, hot tubs, tankless water heaters, ranges, air conditioning, and other service-capacity checks.

Is the electrical load calculator free?

Yes. You can run the calculator for free. If you need help interpreting the result or preparing for permit review, Real World Electric can review the calculation.

Does the calculator replace a permit submission?

No. It helps you estimate and document the electrical load. Permit requirements and final acceptance still depend on the authority having jurisdiction and project details.

Can it fill out the City of Calgary and City of Edmonton forms?

Yes. After you calculate the load, the app can generate a completed City of Calgary or City of Edmonton load calculation form PDF using the selected city, project addresses, dwelling units, calculated watts, amps, breaker size, and conductor size.

What if my result is above my current service size?

You may need load management, a service upgrade, revised equipment choices, or a master electrician review before proceeding.
